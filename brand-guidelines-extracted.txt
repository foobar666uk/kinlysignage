27	Kinly Brand Guidelines
Content
Keep the content clear and concise with minimal clutter. Only
use headers and footers where absolutely necessary. Ensure
that the pages are not text heavy, with plenty of white space
and draw the viewer in.
Where possible keep the amount of colours to a minimum of
two per page, only adding more colours where needed, for
example on graphs.
Standard pages should be white with Deep Purple text.
Statement pages can be Deep Purple with white text. Orange
text is used for CTAs or to make particular text stand out.
Images should fill the pages and not be used as small fillers to
cover up blank areas. Images can be used to convey emotions
or ideas, rather than just technology and meeting rooms.
Where text is placed over an image, ensure that the area
is blurred or flat colour, so that the text is not lost on a
contrasting background.

39	Kinly Brand Guidelines
VIDEO
Size
Videos dimensions should be rectangle at: 1920px X 1080px
Square (1350px X 1350px) is ONLY for Instagram and should be
considered when creating any animation or video.
Intro & Outro Sequences
The intro sequence has a white background, with the Kinly K
opening up and the word, ‘Kinly’ animates out of it. After 2 seconds,
the ‘Kinly’ text fades and the K reduces in size as it goes to the
upper lefthand corner. This is available as a standalone clip that
you can add to your own videos so that you do not need to
re-create it yourself.
The outro in the Intro reversed and is placed at the end. It is
available here. You may not be able to open it on your desktop,
but it does import into Premiere Pro with transparency.
Logo Size and Placement
The Kinly K should stay in the top left hand corner during the full
duration of the video, 80px wide and 50px away from the top and
left edges.
It can be the positive or negative version, depending on the
colour of the background, so use whichever is most visible.
